About Hyderabad

Hyderabad, a vibrant city in India, is known as the City of Pearls for its rich history and cultural heritage. Tourism in Hyderabad has been a major attraction of India due to its blend of traditional and modern elements.Hyderabad has also been recognized for its excellence in tourism. The city won several prestigious awards, including the National Tourism Awards in multiple categories.

CHARMINAR

Charminar is an iconic monument and mosque located in the heart of Hyderabad.Built in 1591, it is known for its four grand arches and minarets. The surrounding markets, especially Laad Bazaar are famous for bangles and pearls.In addition, festivals like Ramadan or Ramazan are celebrated here,and people around the world visit this place all over the year.

GOLCONDA FORT

Golconda Fort in Hyderabad is a historic fortress known for it's brilliant architecture and acoustics. It was the capital of the medieval Golconda Sultanate and offers a panoramic view of the city from its highest point. It is located in western part.There is a secret tunnel in Golconda Fort.

Ramoji Film City

Ramoji Film City is one of the largest film studio complexes in the world. Spread over 2,500 acres, it offers guided tours, amusement parks, and film sets, making it a popular destination for tourists and film enthusiasts.

SALAR JUNG MUSEUM

Salar Jung Museum houses one of the largest collections of art and artifacts by a single person, Mir Yousuf Ali Khan, also known as Salar Jung III. The museum features a diverse range of exhibits from different cultures and eras.

HUSSAIN SAGAR LAKE

Hussain Sagar Lake is a large artificial lake built by Ibrahim Quli Qutb Shah in 1563. It is famous for the monolithic statue of Buddha situated on Gibraltar Rock in the middle of the lake.The lake is a popular spot for boating and evening strolls.

BIRLA MANDIR

Birla Mandir is a stunning temple made of white marble, dedicated to Lord Venkateswara. It is located on a hilltop and offers a serene atmosphere along with views of the city. Situated on a 280-feet high hillock called Naubat Pahad, Birla Mandir offers stunning panoramic views of Hyderabad.

CHOWMAHALLA PALACE

Chowmahalla Palace was the seat of the Asaf Jahi dynasty and is known for its exquisite architecture and royal grandeur. The palace complex consists of four palaces of beautiful gardens.In 2010, Chowmahalla Palace received the UNESCO Asia Pacific Merit Award for Cultural Heritage Conservation.
